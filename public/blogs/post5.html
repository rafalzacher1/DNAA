Protest Advice

If you plan on heading to a protest you must follow the advice of the leading movement of that protest, however, here are some key tips.

What to wear:

  • Nondescript, solid colour, layered clothing (cover any tattoos).
  • A mask (and eye protection if you have access to this).
  • Have your emergency contacts written down (ideally on your arm).
  • Tie up long hair.

What to bring:

  • Water and snacks.
  • ID and cash.
  • Picket signs.
  • Hand sanitiser.

DO NOT bring:

  • Your phone unless you have turned off face/fingerprint ID lock.
  • Jewellery or anything else valuable.
  • Anything you wouldn’t want to have in your possession if you are arrested.
