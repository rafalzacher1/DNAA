Our Manifesto

2020 Highlights

DNA is a student collective for Drug and Alcohol Awareness, aiming to educate and reduce harm and misuse of drugs and alcohol on campus.

  • Short term objectives:

    1. Promote a safer approach to parties and recreational substance use (legal or illegal).
  • 2. Collaborate with existing societies on campus.
  • 3. Collaborate with TEDxRHUL furthering this conversation with industry professionals.

  • Long term objectives:

    1. Create a safer space for users, helping them take control of their choices, and potentially laying a foundation for quitting substance use.
  • 2. Provide resources on how to perform emergency procedures on drug and alcohol related incidents and how to avoid them.
  • 3. Reduce addiction and dependency
  • 3. Educate students on the effects and dangers of commonly used recreational drugs to prevent incidents.
