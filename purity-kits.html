Drug Purity Kits

Stay safe

Our Collective has raised funds to offer drug testing kits as a means of promoting a safer drug environment and reducing harm. 

Following The Black Lives Matter movement, we have decided to match every kit offered with a donation to a BLM charity.

Description

Testing kits serve different purposes.

Ketamine Identification Test

Used for the identification of ketamine, methoxetamine (MXE), N-Ethyl-Ketamine, but also zolpidem (powerful somniferous).

MDMA/ Ecstasy Identification Test

Used for the identification of MDMA (the active principle of ecstasy) on powders and tablets.

Ketamine identification test:

Used for the identification of the main cocaine cutting agents, which can be very dangerous for health. These products include Levamisole, Ephedrine, Phenacetine and Lidocaine.

These test can be done in 4 easy steps:

  • 1. Introduce a small amount of the drug into the test.
  • 2. Introduce the catalyst found in the cap of the test.
  • 3. The small particles or granules in the ampoule (the small sealed glass capsule) will change colour.
  • 4. To examine results, please refer to the colour chart provided in the instructions.
